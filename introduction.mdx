## What is personate.ai

Personate.ai is an AI-driven platform that enables users to create engaging videos efficiently by utilizing customizable AI avatars and hyper-realistic voices in over 60 languages. 

Founded in 2021, Personate.ai has gained prominence for creating India's first AI news anchors, including "Sana" for Aaj Tak, and the government news anchors "Krish" and "Bhoomi" for Doordarshan, delivering vital farming news to rural India in natural, conversational tones.
